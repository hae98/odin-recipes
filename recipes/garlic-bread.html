Garlic Bread

Quick Cheesy Garlic Bread

A very easy recipe for garlic bread that is made with garlic, butter, and Parmesan cheese.

Great side dish to serve along side pasta or roast dinners.

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 loaf crusty bread, split
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Combine garlic, butter, and oil in a microwave safe dish or in a small saucepan. Heat garlic, butter, and oil in microwave for 1 minute or in a small pot over moderate-low heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Toast split bread under broiler. Remove bread when it is toasted golden brown in color. Brush bread liberally with garlic oil. Sprinkle with cheese and parsley.
  3. Return to broiler and brown for 30 seconds. Cut into chunks and serve.
